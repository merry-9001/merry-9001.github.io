右键菜单栏修改

CMD
​

切换为经典旧版右键菜单
​

reg.exe add “HKCU\Software\Classes\CLSID{86ca1aa0-34aa-4e8b-a509-50c905bae2a2}\InprocServer32” /f
​

恢复为现代化新版右键菜单
​

reg.exe delete “HKCU\Software\Classes\CLSID{86ca1aa0-34aa-4e8b-a509-50c905bae2a2}” /f
